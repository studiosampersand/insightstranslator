# Alpha test checklist

## Rewrite workflow

- Enter a rough message and confirm that the result keeps concrete project names, dates and requested outcomes.
- Test `Project loopt uit` and verify that the output is a transformed version of the original, not a generic stock sentence.
- Switch between sender and recipient profiles and confirm that the profile bars visibly change.
- Confirm that every bar length matches its displayed percentage.
- Compare Common ground, Closer to recipient, Closer to me and Neutral.
- Verify that Cool Blue adds factual questions and recommends documentation or a URL.
- Verify that Fiery Red puts the action or decision first.
- Verify that Sunshine Yellow adds a short personal opener and invites input.
- Verify that Earth Green adds alignment, support and team impact.
- Verify that sensitive or complex topics display a call/meeting recommendation separately from the written reply.

## Layout

- Desktop: original text stays left and rewrite stays right.
- Tablet: cards remain readable without horizontal overflow.
- Mobile: input, output and profile bridge stack vertically.
- Output remains editable and can be copied.

## Ask Liv

- IT hardware/infrastructure routes to HaloPSA.
- HR and vehicle-fleet questions route to Maarten.
- CRM, DMS, project capacity and collaboration questions route to the expected service category.

## Boundaries

- No real employee profiles in the public GitHub demo.
- Screenshot is preview-only; no OCR is claimed.
- No message is automatically sent.
- The deterministic alpha engine is not presented as a fully trained internal agent.
